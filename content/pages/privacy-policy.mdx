## Introduction

Welcome to Nano Banana Studio, an AI-powered image generation and editing platform. At Nano Banana Studio, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Nano Banana Studio:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account via Google OAuth.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, track your usage, and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Nano Banana Studio, including the number of images generated, styles used, and feature interactions.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our services, and manage subscription limits.

3. **Generated Content**
   - **What We Collect**: The prompts you submit and images you generate or upload for editing.
   - **Purpose**: To provide the image generation and editing services you request.
   - **Note**: We do not use your generated content to train our AI models.

4. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access our service, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Payment Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Payment details processed through our third-party payment provider (Creem.io).
   - **Purpose**: To process subscription payments securely.
   - **Note**: We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely, and we implement security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access.

Your generated images are temporarily stored for delivery purposes and are not retained long-term unless you explicitly save them to your account.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others
- To Google (for authentication via Google OAuth)
- To our payment processor (Creem.io) for subscription management

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Export your data in a portable format

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
