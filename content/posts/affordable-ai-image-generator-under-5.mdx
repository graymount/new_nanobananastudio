## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there a cheap AI image generator with no signup?

Most require at least a Google or email sign-in. Stable Diffusion is fully open but requires your own GPU hardware and technical setup.

### Under $5/month: how many images do you actually get?

It depends on the tool. Nano Banana Starter gives 50 images/month at $4.95. Free tiers vary from 25/day (with queuing) to unlimited (self-hosted). Check each tool's current pricing page.

### What is the catch with cheap AI image generators?

Common trade-offs include lower monthly image limits, slower generation during peak hours, fewer style options, and less reliable text rendering. See [how text rendering quality compares across generators](/blog/ai-text-rendering-guide).

### Which cheap AI image generator is best for product photos?

For e-commerce product mockups, look for tools that support image-to-image editing, not just text-to-image. This lets you refine existing product photos rather than generating from scratch.

### Which cheap option handles text and logos best?

Text rendering accuracy varies significantly. Budget tools generally struggle with long text. For text-heavy images, test with your actual use case before committing to a paid plan.

### Do cheap AI generators produce lower quality images?

Not necessarily. Image quality depends more on the underlying model than the price. Some budget tools use the same foundation models as premium services.

### Can I use cheap AI-generated images commercially?

Check each tool's terms. Most paid plans include commercial usage rights. Free tiers often have restrictions on commercial use or require attribution.
