## How It Actually Works

Nano Banana is a purpose-built frontend for Google's Gemini image generation model. Three steps:

1. **You write a prompt** — describe the image you want, put any text in "quotation marks"
2. **Gemini interprets it** — maps your words to visual concepts: composition, style, objects, text content
3. **Image generates in ~5 seconds** — download it, save to gallery, or use as reference for the next one

Same underlying technology as other AI tools. The difference is focus: Nano Banana is built around **text-heavy use cases** — anything where the words in the image actually matter.

| Scenario | Other tools | Nano Banana |

No AI tool is reliable on everything. Long sentences break. Cursive breaks. But on the use cases that actually matter for most people — short text on signs, logos, labels, social graphics — the difference is noticeable.
