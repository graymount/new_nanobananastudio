## What is Nano Banana?

Nano Banana is an AI-powered image generator and editor built on **Google's Gemini AI technology**. According to [Google's official documentation](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/imagen), Gemini's image generation capabilities enable natural language understanding for visual creation.

With Nano Banana, you can:

- **Create images from text** - Describe what you want, and AI generates it
- **Edit photos with words** - Tell the AI what to change in your existing images
- **Apply artistic styles** - Transform images into cyberpunk, anime, watercolor, and more

## How to Create Images from Text

Follow these steps to generate your first AI image:

1. **Select "Text-to-Image" mode** in the generator
2. **Write a detailed prompt** describing your desired image
3. **Choose a style** (optional) - Select from presets like Cyberpunk, Miyazaki, Realistic, etc.
4. **Click Generate** - Wait a few seconds for your AI image

### Writing Effective Prompts

The key to great results is writing good prompts. Based on [best practices from AI research](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.07459), here are proven tips:

**Be Specific**
- ❌ Bad: "a cat"
- ✅ Good: "a fluffy orange cat sitting on a windowsill at sunset"

**Include Style Details**
- Add lighting: "soft morning light", "neon glow"
- Mention mood: "peaceful", "dramatic", "mysterious"
- Specify art style: "oil painting style", "digital art", "photorealistic"

**Example Prompts to Try**

Try these prompts in [Nano Banana Studio](/app):

```
A cozy coffee shop interior with warm lighting,
rain visible through the window, watercolor style
```

```

and neon signs, cyberpunk style
```

```

```

## How to Edit Photos with Nano Banana

1. **Select "Image Editing" mode**
2. **Upload your image** - Drag and drop or click to browse
3. **Describe the changes** you want in natural language
4. **Click Generate** to see the edited result

### Example Editing Commands

- "Change the background to a beach sunset"
- "Make the person wear a red dress instead"
- "Add snow falling in the scene"
- "Transform this into anime style"
- "Remove the background and make it transparent"

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many images can I create for free?
Free accounts get 8 generations per month. Generations reset at the beginning of each billing cycle.

### What image formats are supported?
PNG, JPG, and WEBP for both input and output.

### Can I use the images commercially?
Free tier images are for personal use only. Upgrade to Pro for commercial rights.

### How long does generation take?
Most images are generated in 5-15 seconds, depending on complexity.

### Is Nano Banana safe to use?
Yes. We use secure authentication via Google OAuth and don't store your images on our servers longer than necessary for processing.
